About BuenoSpace

BuenoSpace is a place where people can list, find and rent beautiful and special spaces

BuenoSpace is designed to allow people to rent spaces for events and allows property owners to rent out spaces for a profit. By using BuenoSpace, we believe that there will be an increase in demand for individuals and companies to organise events following the improved COVID-19 situation in the country. Thus, boosting the gig economy as it allows space owners from all over Malaysia to list their event space in order to earn extra income.
